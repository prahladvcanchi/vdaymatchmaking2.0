# vdaymatchmaking: FindYourMatch

Automated Valentine’s day matchmaking via an application that finds the top five Valentine matches for every participating student at my school and matched 500+ students in <2 seconds. Used for Love Week 2026
at Mission San Jose High School in partnership with @LiLockedIn's Scavenger Hunt and as promotion for the upcoming Winter Ball.
